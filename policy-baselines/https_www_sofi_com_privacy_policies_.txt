SoFi’s Privacy Policies
SoFi is a new kind of finance company taking a member centric approach to financial services. We take the privacy of our customers seriously, and the policies outlined below describe how we use and secure their personal information. For additional detail please review these policies to learn about how we collect, disclose, and use information we collect or receive about you.
